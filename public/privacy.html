Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

BillFighter ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using BillFighter (the "Service"), you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little as possible. Specifically:

  • Bill content you submit. When you upload a file or paste text from a medical bill, that content is sent to our servers solely to extract the text and analyze it. We do not store your uploaded files or bill text after your analysis is complete — it is processed in memory and discarded.
  • Payment information. When you purchase a dispute letter, payment is processed by our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, who acts as the Merchant of Record. We never see or store your full card number. Lemon Squeezy collects the information necessary to process your payment under their own privacy policy.
  • Basic technical data. Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps for security and performance.

2. How we use information

  • To analyze your medical bill and generate your results and dispute letter.
  • To process your payment and deliver the product you purchased.
  • To maintain the security, reliability, and performance of the Service.
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

3. Medical information

Medical bills may contain sensitive information. We treat the content you submit as confidential. We do not sell, rent, or share your bill content with third parties. Bill content is used only to produce your analysis and letter, and is not retained afterward. Please avoid including information you do not wish to be processed (for example, you may redact your Social Security number before uploading).

4. How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate the Service, and only to the extent necessary:

  • AI processing provider — to analyze the text of your bill and generate the letter.
  • Lemon Squeezy — to process payments as our Merchant of Record.
  • Hosting provider — to serve the website and run our functions.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

5. Data retention

Uploaded files and bill text are not stored after processing. Payment records are retained by Lemon Squeezy as required for accounting and legal compliance. Technical logs are retained for a limited period for security purposes.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. Because we do not retain your bill content, most of this data no longer exists once your session ends. For payment-related data, contact Lemon Squeezy or us and we will assist. To make a request, email support@billfighter.app.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect information in transit (for example, encrypted HTTPS connections). However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@billfighter.app.
